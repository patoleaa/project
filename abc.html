THE GREAT INDIA

India Before/after

India (Bharat, Hindustan), South Asia ka ek desh hai. Iski abaadi 1,370,000,000 se jaada hai. India ki raajdhaani New Delhi hai. Is desh ki 22 official languages hain. Ye area me duniya ke seventh sab se bada desh hai aur abaadi mein dusra sab se bada desh hai, lekin duniya ki sab se badi democracy hai. Iske south me Indian Ocean, west me Arabian Sea, east me Bay of Bengal hai. Iske border ke paschim me Pakistan, uttar me China, Bhutan, Nepal aur poorab me Bangladesh aur Myanmar hai. India ke dakshin me Indian Ocean me to the east. India ke dakshin me Sri Lanka, Maldives, aur Indonesia hai.
